## §0 Honest scope fence

This is a multi-disease pilot CROSS-TAB. The 200-disease re-mapping
the `AXIS/HIERARCHY.tape` Log flags as deferred work remains
deferred — these cells are read from the small cohort of
case-study portfolios actually present in `case_studies/`. Every
cell that holds an FDA drug name was read from the originating
portfolio runner's own witness JSON (`in_scope_drugs[].drug` or
`drug_name`). No drug is invented; no axis mapping is invented.
The cell glyph legend is:

- **`✓ <drug-name>`** — IN-SCOPE FDA-approved drug on this axis.
- **`🔬 research`** — research-stage candidate listed on this axis
  in a portfolio's `research_stage_negatives[]` (or a
  clinical-stage drug in `in_scope_drugs[]` flagged
  `fda_approval_status='clinical_stage'`).
- **`⚠ UNPLACED`** — a CBER / CDRH scope-disqualified row from a
  portfolio's `not_in_scope_drugs[]` (or a research-stage
  negative with `fda_center_if_filed='CBER'/'CDRH'` and a null
  `axis_in_repo`). These do NOT bind to a specific axis column
  by design — listing them at the disease level keeps the
  honesty discipline of criterion #4 (drug-only / CDER scope).
- **`·`** — no candidate / not applicable (honest empty cell).
